The Turtle Species

A Journey Through Chelonian Diversity

Welcome to the Turtle Species project! The order Testudines, which encompasses turtles, tortoises, and terrapins, represents one of the oldest reptile groups, with a lineage stretching back over 200 million years. From the vast, open ocean to small, freshwater streams and dry, arid land, these remarkable creatures have evolved specialized adaptations to thrive in diverse environments. Explore the five fascinating species below—including both aquatic sea turtles and terrestrial tortoises—to dive into their unique biology, ecological roles, and conservation challenges through interactive slide presentations.
