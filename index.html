The Turtle Species

Welcome to the comprehensive guide to five fascinating turtle species. Turtles, ancient reptiles that have roamed the Earth for millions of years, represent incredible diversity, from the vast, migratory **sea turtles** to the burrow-dwelling **tortoises** and the specialized **freshwater turtles**. This collection of interactive slide presentations explores the unique biology, habitats, and conservation status of the Kemp's Ridley Sea Turtle, Loggerhead Sea Turtle, Gopher Tortoise, Flattened Musk Turtle, and the Alabama Red-bellied Turtle. Dive in to learn more about these remarkable creatures and the pioneers who helped bring them to scientific light.
